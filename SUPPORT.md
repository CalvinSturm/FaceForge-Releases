# FaceForge Support

For public alpha support, open an issue:
[FaceForge Releases issues](https://github.com/CalvinSturm/FaceForge-Releases/issues)

## Before You Post

Do not upload private videos, source faces, IDs, documents, workplace footage,
medical footage, footage involving minors, or other sensitive media.

Use a test clip, redacted screenshot, or written description when possible.

## Issue Template

```text
FaceForge version:
Windows version:
GPU model:
NVIDIA driver version, if known:

What I tried:

What happened:

What I expected:

Any error message:

Screenshot attached? Yes/No
```

## Helpful Details

- Whether the app launched successfully.
- Whether the issue happened during startup, preview, export, or model loading.
- Whether you used the default `Blur Faces / Privacy` workflow.
- Whether Windows SmartScreen or antivirus blocked any files.
- Whether the app reported missing models.

Do not post full logs without reviewing them first. Logs may include system
details, local paths, settings, or other information you may not want public.
