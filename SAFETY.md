# FaceForge Safety Policy

FaceForge is primarily for privacy-preserving face blur and video redaction.

Use FaceForge to protect identities, remove or obscure faces, and prepare
privacy-safer videos before sharing.

## Acceptable Use

- Blurring faces in videos you have the right to edit.
- Redacting bystanders, customers, students, employees, or private individuals.
- Preparing privacy-safe clips for support, training, publishing, or review.
- Testing face replacement only with clear consent and rights to both source
  and target media.

## Face Replacement

Face replacement is an advanced, consent-based Pro workflow. You must have
consent and rights for both:

- the source face or identity being used
- the target video or image being edited

Some platforms require disclosure for realistic AI-altered media. You are
responsible for following the rules that apply to your use.

## Unacceptable Use

Do not use FaceForge for:

- Nonconsensual media.
- Impersonation.
- Harassment, intimidation, or abuse.
- Public-figure deception.
- Explicit or sexual content involving real people.
- Minors.
- Fraud, scams, or misleading identity use.
- Editing media you do not have rights to use.
- Evading platform rules, disclosure requirements, or legal obligations.

## Public Sharing

Before sharing edited media, review the result carefully. Make sure faces are
sufficiently blurred or redacted for your privacy goal, and disclose AI
alteration when required by law, policy, or platform rules.
